!!! warning "Community servers are best-effort demos"
    The public OVOS servers exist for easy onboarding and demos only. They are best-effort,
    not optimized, carry no uptime guarantees, and may vanish at any time. OVOS will be slow
    and unreliable if you rely on them. The official recommendation is to self-host — or skip
    servers entirely: fully offline plugins exist for everything.
